# Rules

## Objective

Be the first team to reach **200 points** across multiple rounds.

---

## Setup

- Uses a double-six set (28 tiles)
- 4 players split into **2 teams of 2** — partners sit across from each other
- Each player draws **7 tiles** (all 28 tiles are dealt — no boneyard)

---

## Starting the Game

- The player holding the **[6|6]** plays first and must play it
- In subsequent rounds, the **winner of the previous round** starts

---

## Gameplay

- Play proceeds **clockwise**
- On your turn, place a tile that matches an open end of the chain
- Doubles are placed **perpendicular** to the chain
- If you can't play, you **pass**
- If all 4 players pass consecutively, the game is **blocked**

---

## Winning a Round

- **Domino:** First player to play all their tiles wins the round for their team
- **Blocked game:** The team with the lower combined pip count wins
- **Blocked game tie:** No points are awarded; the next round starts with whoever won the previous round

---

## Scoring

- The winning team scores the **total pip count of both opponents' remaining tiles**
- In a blocked game, the winning team scores the **difference** in pip counts between the two teams
- First team to **200 points** wins the match

---

## Team Strategy

- Partners sit across from each other and work together
- Infer your partner's hand from their plays
- Block opponents while opening lines for your partner
